Background

I have a passion for every art form and writing software is another means to that end. Specifically, writing front-end code that uses either CSS Materialize, Bootstrap, React and soon to be SASS. While I love the frontend I also have backend experience with Node JS and SQL. Using Git on the command line is something that is increasingly becoming my favorite tool.

I lost my passion for the film industry when I met my future self in L.A. whom was editing The Bachelor for 18 hours a day on minimum pay. I believe in working hard but maintaining a healthy lifestyle is equally important. I enjoy rock climbing, critiquing art and playing board games with friends and family.
